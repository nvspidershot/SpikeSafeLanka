About Us

Dedicated to Safer Communities Across Sri Lanka

SpikeSafe Lanka is a non-profit initiative committed to combating drink spiking through education, research, and community engagement. We work closely with local authorities, healthcare providers, and educational institutions to create safer environments for everyone.

  • Comprehensive Database

    Access verified resources and research specific to Sri Lankan context

  • Anonymous Reporting

    Secure, confidential incident reporting with no personal data collection

  • Community Support

    Connecting victims with local support services and counseling

  • Prevention Education

    Workshops and materials for venues, universities, and organizations
